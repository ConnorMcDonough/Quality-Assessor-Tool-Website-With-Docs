This website quality assessor tool will give you a score for the quality of your website. Each question is rated on a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is strongly disagree and 5 is strongly agree. So if you find that a question applies to your website, then choose 'strongly agree'. If it does not apply at all, click 'strongly disagree'. If your website is somewhere in between, then we have some more neutral options as well. If you have any qustions please visit our Help page.
