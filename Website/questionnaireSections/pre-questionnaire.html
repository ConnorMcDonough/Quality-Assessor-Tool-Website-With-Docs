This is the page where you will begin the Website Quality Assessor questionnaire. This tool will help you assess if your website is up to standards. There are nine sections of three questions. The sections in the questionnaire are: Content, Function, Structure, Usability, Navigability, Performance, Compatibility, Interoperability, and Security. You will be given the options "strongly agree", "agree", "neutral", "disagree", "strongly agree", and "not applicable". You will see your score at the end. Hopefully, the results help you improve your website. Additional help can be found on the Help page.
